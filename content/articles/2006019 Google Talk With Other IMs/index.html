--- title: Google Talk With Other IMs date: 2006-01-19 10:33:00 Z tags: ["Tech","Google","Talk","IM"] wordpress_path: /2006/01/google-talk-with-other-ims --- Google Talk has been opened to communicate with other XMPP IM services! One of the complaints against it when it first launched was that it used the open XMPP protocol, but was a closed system. No longer. This is a smart, and a useful, move for them. Hopefully they'll add MSN/AIM/ICQ integration eventaully as well...
