--- title: Standardised RSS date: 2006-06-14 11:24:00 Z tags: ["XOXOBlog","Feeds","Converting"] wordpress_path: /2006/06/standardised-rss --- Standard RSS is a tool which takes any RSS, ATOM, hAtom, or XOXO Blog Format feed and turns it into a standard RSS 2.0 core. More information is available on the tool's home page. There is also a YubNub command to go with it.
