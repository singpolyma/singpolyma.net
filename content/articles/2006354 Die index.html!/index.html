--- title: Die index.html! date: 2006-12-20 22:38:00 Z tags: ["Tech"] wordpress_path: /2006/12/die-indexhtml --- It has annoyed me for some time that the default 'Home' links on the new Blogger (which I will continue to call Blogger BETA, at least for today, don't worry, I know that's officially wrong) go to /index.html. Some internal links ('View Blog' from the dashboard) do not, which is worse. Why have a URL longer than it need be? Why have multiple URLs available that people can bookmark? Doesn't that just confuse things. This hack is very simple and fixes the problem.
