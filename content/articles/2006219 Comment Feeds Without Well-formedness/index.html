--- title: Comment Feeds Without Well-formedness date: 2006-08-07 14:52:00 Z tags: ["Tech","Comments","Blogger","XML"] wordpress_path: /2006/08/comment-feeds-without-well-formedness --- In response to the popular confusion about XML well-formedness and a recent nudgeing by Greg, I have upgraded Blogger Recent Comments. People who have been there before will note that there is now one less instruction -- XML well-formedness is no longer necessary! I have tested this with a Blogger template on which I purposely broke well-formedness and the comments still came through fine! Originally introduced on this blog, Blogger Recent Comments is a Ning app that can automatially generate RSS, JavaScript, and JSON feeds of all comments on your blog. Setup is now just three easy steps!
